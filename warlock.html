WARLOCK class

The warlock class is a very independent and versatile class. The greatest reason for this is the SunSinger specification. One of the last unlocks grant you the "Fireborn" ability, that gives you the ability to come back from death all on your own. Their two damage tiers are Solar and Void so keep that in mind when it comes to weekly challenges.

Class Recommendation

From Level 1 you start off putting points into Void. I would immediately switch that to SunSinger so that you upgrade that as fast as possible, having a fire-born revive makes you just about the most marketable type of Guardian in the game so getting groups will be very easy, whereas if you put your points into Void you'll have high damage output but that can be easily matched or exceeded with the other classes.
