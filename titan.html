Titan class

You would think just based on the name that the Titan class would be a "tank" class that can just be a damage sponge. This isn't really the case, they can take and deal out punishment but only marginally better than the other two classes. Titans are more of a support class with their shields and short range-high damage super.

Class Recommendation

I would focus on the Defender sub-class, since you get a shield early on this helps you out with tougher solo quests, and similar to the Warlocks this makes you very marketable for high level groups since you can save everyone's bacon at a moment's notice.
