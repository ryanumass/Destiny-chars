Vault of Glass

The Vault of Glass is the first Raid in Destiny. There are currently two difficulties: Normal (Lvl 26) and Hard (Lvl 30). I recommend being at least level 27 before attempting Vault to Glass. You need 5 other people to succeed and the enemies become increasingly difficult as the Raid progresses so to avoid frustration and heartbreak after hours invested definitely spend the extra time to level up before hand.

The hard version of the raid entails everything the normal one does but additionally you can't respawn your teammates until you finish each checkpoint. Since the normal raid takes about 3+ hours with a couple team members that haven't done it before just try and extrapolate that with no respawns and harder enemies.

Also, since the latest update the Gear that you have to work really hard for is now obsolete for higher levels so stick with normal Vanguard quests and bounties if you have the option. But VOG is a lot of fun so everyone should try it at least once.

Release: September 15, 2014

Pros:
1 Really fun experience with friends
2 Opportunity for Exotic Weapons
3 Ascendant Materials

Cons:
1 Gear isn't as relevant with DLC release
2 Need expensive materials to upgrade exotic weapons to higher level
3 Harder to get people to commit with Crota's End release

Personal Recommendation

VOG is still worth doing if you have a decent team. You get a decent amount of accendant materials that's still relevant to level up any high level gear and it doesn't take long at all now with an experienced team. You can get exotic weapons that are still the best in the game and they can be upgraded later on. Stay away from hard mode unless you really need something to do.

The time invested and chance for failure makes it barely worth the effort unless you're over lvl 30 and know the people you're playing with. The Gear that you have to work really hard for is now obsolete for higher levels so stick with normal Vanguard quests and bounties if you have the option. But VOG is a lot of fun so everyone should try it at least once.
