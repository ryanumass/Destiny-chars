Raids

Crota's End

This is the new raid launched by Destiny. Currently there is only one difficulty, but one is definitely enough for now. This raid takes place on the moon and takes the level of team work even further by coonstantly punishing your team with timed events and challenges.

Release: December 9, 2014
Pros:
1 Super new Raid...always fun
2 New Gear and content
3 Ominous parts of main story finally making some sense
4 You can solo the first chest!

Cons:
1 HARD....like really HAAARRRDD
2 Elitists choosey about who they'll raid with on account of the level of difficulty
3 Brand New Raid...so lots of unknowns. Mistakes will be made

Personal Recommendation

If you're not level 30 you'll probably just get in the way, so unless you have maxed out Icebreaker or Gallahorn weapons I wouldn't even bother trying to find a group. This is a serious time commitment and there's a lot left to explore so if you're easily frustrated or can't commit atleast 3 hours I would stick to other things.

But! At the very least you should spent 10 minutes to get the first chest, it's been known to contain raid armor, weapons, and high level materials and it's in the very first part of the Raid. Not bad for a chance to get the very best stuff in the game.
